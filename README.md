# Mega-Man-Zero-Clone

A project developed for multiple assignments in the course Gameplay Programming 2. This project is a clone of the Megaman Zero games.

![Gameplay1](Media/Gameplay1.gif)

## Gameplay

Upon starting the game from the title screen, a 90 second countdown to beat the level will begin.

There are 4 enemy types.

A goblin that will approach and attack you.

Floating orbs that will approach and try to damage you.

A stationary turret that shoots arrows.

A boss that periodically spawns the second enemy type a casts an aoe orb around itself.

There's 3 sections to the level.

A flat area with a single goblin.

A platforming area with a hazard below, a turret enemy, and a lot of the floating orb enemies.

And a boss arena that closes when you enter. It had moving flying cubes that you have to ride to attack the boss.

![Gameplay2](Media/Gameplay2.gif)

## Controls

A & LArrow: Move left

D & RArrow: Move right

W, UArrow, & Space: Jump

E: Shoot

Hold E: Charge and shoot

Release E (After Charging): Stronger shot

Q: Shield

F: Swing Sword

G: Jump Attack

C: Spin Attack

Hold R: Cast Magic AoE

## Limitations

The movement doesn't feel very good.
The collision detection leaves something to be desired.
The level isn't very scenic or pretty.
